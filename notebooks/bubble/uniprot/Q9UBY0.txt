CC   -!- FUNCTION: Plasma membrane Na(+)/H(+) antiporter. Mediates the
CC       electroneutral exchange of intracellular H(+) ions for extracellular
CC       Na(+) (PubMed:10444453). Major apical Na(+)/H(+) exchanger in the base
CC       of the colonic crypt. Controls in the colonic crypt intracellular pH
CC       (pHi) to direct colonic epithelial cell differentiation into the
CC       absorptive enterocyte lineage at the expense of the secretory lineage
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q3ZAS0,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10444453}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=H(+)(out) + Na(+)(in) = H(+)(in) + Na(+)(out);
CC         Xref=Rhea:RHEA:29419, ChEBI:CHEBI:15378, ChEBI:CHEBI:29101;
CC         Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:10444453};
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with CHP1 and CHP2. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P48763}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Apical cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8772498}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in skeletal muscle, colon and kidney.
CC       Lower levels in the testis, prostate, ovary, and small intestine
CC       (PubMed:10444453, PubMed:8843774). In the distal colon, expressed along
CC       the cryptal axis (PubMed:8843774). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10444453,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8843774}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the monovalent cation:proton antiporter 1 (CPA1)
CC       transporter (TC 2.A.36) family. {ECO:0000305}.

CC       domains in the Na(+)/H(+) exchangers vary among authors. {ECO:0000305}.
