CC   -!- FUNCTION: Essential for male fertility and sperm mitochondrial sheath
CC       formation (By similarity). Required for proper mitochondrial elongation
CC       and coiling along the flagellum during the formation of the
CC       mitochondrial sheath (By similarity). Facilitates the growth and
CC       aggressiveness of neuroblastoma cells (PubMed:30026490). Increases the
CC       EZH2 activity and H3K27me3 levels in a RBBP4-dependent manner, and
CC       facilitates the enrichment of polycomb repressive complex 2 and
CC       H3K27me3 on gene promoters, resulting in transcriptional repression of
CC       tumor suppressors affecting the proliferation, invasion, and metastasis
CC       of tumor cells (PubMed:30026490). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80X86,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:30026490}.
